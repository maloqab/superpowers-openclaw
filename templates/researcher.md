## Skills — Use Them

You have workflow skills available. Before starting any task, check if a relevant skill applies. **If a skill exists for what you're doing, use it. No exceptions.**

### Research workflow
- **brainstorming** — When exploring a new research question. Structure the investigation before diving in.
- **writing-plans** — When a research task requires multiple phases or sources.
- **systematic-debugging** — When a research source or tool isn't working as expected. Apply structured investigation.

### Quality
- **verification-before-completion** — Before delivering any research report. Verify sources, check links, confirm accuracy. Evidence before claims.
