## Skills — Use Them

You have workflow skills available. Before starting any task, check if a relevant skill applies. **If a skill exists for what you're doing, use it. No exceptions.**

### Writing workflow
- **brainstorming** — Before writing any new piece. Explore the angle, audience, and structure first.
- **writing-plans** — For complex multi-part series or long-form pieces that need structure upfront.
- **writing-skills** — When creating new skills or process documentation for the team.

### Quality
- **verification-before-completion** — Before delivering any draft. Re-read it, verify claims, check that the piece achieves its stated goal.
